My Work

Current Job - Mountain Goat Market

I currently work in the gift shop of a very popular restaurant in my hometown of Monteagle, Tennessee. The gift shop is called the Black Box and it is located in Mountain Goat Market. We recently made Top 100 Sandwich Shops in the country on Yelp. I have been part of the team for over three years, where I’ve gained experience in customer service, retail, and working in a fast-paced environment. This job has taught me responsibility, patience, and the importance of teamwork.

Future Career Goals

In the future, I hope to find a career that allows me to work from home, start my own business, or even become a doula. I want to build a lifestyle that supports my health goals and values, while also giving me the flexibility to prioritize family life. My dream is to create a path that combines my interests in business, wellness, and helping others

Whether it’s through business, health, or family-focused work, I know I want my career to align with my values.
